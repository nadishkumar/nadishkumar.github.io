Key Highlights and Specifications



Processor

8 - core AMD Ryzen Zen 2-architecture CPU at maximum 3.5GHz

Graphics

AMD Navi/RDNA 2-family GPU with 36 CU at 2.23GHz

Video Memory

16GB GDDR6 with 256-bit interface (448GBps)

Storage

825GB SSD at 5.5-9GBps; NVMe SSD slot; support for USB HDD


Optical Drive, Resolution

4K Blu-ray Optical drive with maximum output resolution of 8K and maximum frame rate of 4K/120fps


Audio

3D, accelerated by custom Tempest Engine hardware; for headphones only at launch, supplemented by virtual surround for speaker audio

New Controller Features

Haptic feedback, adaptive triggers, USB-C connector



Notable Games

Assassin's Creed Valhalla, Godfall, Gods and Monsters, Observer: System Redux, Rainbow Six Siege, Spider-Man: Miles Morales, Watch Dogs: Legion
